Hello. This is

Xinmin Chorale

Xinmin Chorale is a youth Choir consisting of talented and enthusiastic singers. We seek to inculcate a passion for singing in each of our choristers, and in the process mould them into all-round and gracious student leaders.

Rules and Expectations for Choir Members

Time for Choir Sessions

We have choir sessions every Wednesday, from 3.45pm to 6.00, and every Friday, from 2.00pm to 5.00pm.

If you cannot come for one of the days, please inform your SL ahead of time (as soon as possible). Please remember that you are expected to submit a Medical Certificate for your absence from practices.

Please do not be late for choir sessions!

Attire

On Wednesdays, we wear either school uniform or PE. On Fridays, we wear Choir Tee and school skirt/school shorts/school pants.

Please do take note of the attire for these two days!

What to bring for Choir

For every choir session, you have to bring a

- a choir file

- a water bottle

- a pen

During choir sessions

During choir sessions, turn your mobile phones to silent mode and put them in your bag. You are not allowed to use them during choir practices.

Please DO NOT talk amongst yourselves excessively, be attentive to the conductor/section leader and try your best to sing!

What our students
think about Choir

John (Sec 4, Bass)

Choir's a place I call home, and the members are closely bonded. We don't just sing here in Choir, we have all sorts of activities, from bonding activities in school to camp that involves leaving the school venue. Here in Choir, we are all inclusive, loving, caring and last but certainly not the least, family.

Amos (Sec 3, Tenor)

Choir has been a wonderful journey for me. When I came into Choir, I was greeted with open arms and was welcomed by everyone in Choir. My section leaders were diligent and resilient while my conductor was kind and had a good sense of humor. Choir has also been like a second home for me, with caring students and teachers that comfort and support us when any of us felt down.

Erin (Sec 4, Alto)

Choir has certainly been an extremely fruitful experience for me. Not only was I able to make new friends, I was also able to develop my leadership skills and grow as a person. Choir is like a second family to me and I will always remember the lessons that Choir has taught me. Being part of Choir exco, has helped me step out of my comfort zone and feel more at ease talking in front of a crowd. It has also taught me how to work with different types of people and plan various events such as supernova, Choir camp and other bonding activities. Moreover, Choir has helped me to develop my passion for singing and I will always remember the happiness that I feel when I perform on stage with the other Choir members. Although practices may be tiring at times, I believe that it has certainly helped develop our perseverance and communication skills. All in all, my experience in Choir has definitely been fulfilling and I will always be grateful to be part of the xinmin chorale family!

Feeza (Sec 4, Soprano)

Choir is about teamwork, dedication and passion for singing. Like the saying, 'One for all, All for one'. When the time goes rough, Choir was always another place for me to sing my emotions out. Lastly, Choir is definitely where you can feel a sense of achievement after a long practice.
